Voici la liste des dons supplémentaires d'animanthrope, qui s'ajoute aux dons existants (voir [PF2e SRD](https://pf2e.pathfinder-fr.org/ancestry-feats?ancestry=beastkin)).

```dataview
LIST
FROM "Règles/PF2/Dons"
WHERE type = "pf2-feat"
  AND pf2-feat-category = "heritage"
  AND contains(pf2-traits, "animanthrope")
SORT pf2-level ASC, file.name ASC
```
